How to grow daffodils?

Daffodils do best in full sun, though they will grow in partial shade. They're generally not picky about soil, but good drainage is key. They are susceptible to rot when kept too wet, so make sure that you plant them in a well-draining spot. Hillsides and raised beds work well.
