1. Login

To access your account, you need to have a registered account with us.

1.1 Enter the email address associated with your account. This should be the email address you used during the registration process.

1.2 Provide your password in the designated field. Make sure to enter the exact password you used when creating your account. Note that passwords are case-sensitive.

1.3 Click on the "Login" button.

2. Sign Up

To create an account, please follow these steps:

2.1 Enter your full name in the designated field.

2.2 Provide a valid email address. This will be used for communication and account verification.

2.3 Choose a unique username for your account. It should be memorable and easy to identify.

2.4 Create a strong password for your account. Make sure it is a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters.

2.5 Click on the "Sign Up" button to complete the registration process.

3. Home Page

Welcome to our home page! Here's what you can expect:

3.1 Description Section: Get an overview of our website and what we offer.

3.2 Client Image Slider: Explore a slideshow of images showcasing satisfied clients and their cultures.

3.3 Features Section: Discover the key features and benefits of our product or service.

4. Account Page

On the account page, you can manage your personal information and account settings. Here's what you'll find:

4.1 Personal Information: View your name, username, and email associated with your account.

4.2 Edit Profile: Update your name, username, and password as needed. Make sure to choose a strong and secure password.

4.3 Delete Account: If you wish to remove your account, this section allows you to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

5. My Cultures

In the "My Cultures" dashboard, you can manage and explore your cultural experiences. Here's what you'll find:

5.1 Culture List: View a list of all your cultures, including their details and images.

5.2 Add New Culture: Create a new culture entry by providing the necessary details, such as name, description, and associated sensors.

7. New Culture

On the "Buy Page," you can explore cultures that other players have put up for sale. Here's what you can do:

8.1 View Cultures for Sale: Browse through a list of cultures available for purchase.

8.2 Buy or Follow Cultures: Click on a culture to view its details. You can choose to buy the culture or follow it for updates.
